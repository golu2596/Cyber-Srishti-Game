Accomodation

Accommodation to the guest would be provided in the campus of JIIT Noida and it will be purely on a shared basis. These facilities would be separate for girls and boys. Charges for accommodation are INR 1800 + 100(Cautionary amount) per head for three days that is 16th, 17th and 18th December. To avail an accommodation for 15th December an extra amount of INR 300 will be charged and rooms will be allotted from 14th December 4 PM onwards. Accommodation facility would be made available from 4 PM on 15th of December, 2016 onwards and you would be required to vacate the room on or before 10 AM on 19th December, 2016.
